Name job: Production Team Leader

Location: Ninh Binh province, Viet Nam

Duration: 07/08 — 08/09/2024

Salary range: Between 9,000,000 and 12,000,000

Responsibilities:

  • Lead and motivate a team of production associates to achieve daily production targets.
  • Implement and monitor production schedules to optimize efficiency and minimize downtime.
  • Ensure adherence to quality standards and safety regulations.
  • Conduct regular performance evaluations and provide coaching and development to team members.
  • Identify and implement process improvements to enhance productivity and reduce costs.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to resolve production issues and drive continuous improvement initiatives.

Requirements:

  • Bachelor's degree in Engineering, Operations Management, or a related field.
  • Minimum of 3 years of experience in a supervisory role within a manufacturing environment.
  • Proven ability to lead and motivate teams.
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
  • Proficiency in using production management software and tools.

What we offer

  • Competitive salary and benefits package.
  • Opportunities for professional growth and development.
  • A dynamic and collaborative work environment.
  • Be part of a company committed to sustainability and innovation.
