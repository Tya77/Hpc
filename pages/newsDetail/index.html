On January 16, 2023, VNK’s International Polybag,.JSC held the 2022 closing ceremony and implemented the 2023 plan at K'S VN factory, in Ninh Binh province.

The ceremony was attended by the Provincial Labor Confederation, Ninh Binh Industrial Park Union, Board of Directors and employees of the Company. At the ceremony, on behalf of the Board of Directors, Company Director Nguyen Thi Giang summarized the activities achieved as well as set out plans and goals for production and business activities in 2023.

In the past year, despite facing general difficulties due to the impact of the economic situation at home and abroad, under the timely transformation direction of the company's board of directors, as well as the solidarity and efforts of the entire The company's employees have achieved certain successes.

Also at the Closing Ceremony, the company's Board of Directors set new production and business goals for 2023, encouraging and promoting creative improvement ideas in production and business activities of the company.

Employees, focusing on training and developing high quality human resources.
